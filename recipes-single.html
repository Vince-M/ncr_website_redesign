10 - Minute Cheesy Pasta

Comfy and filling, this easy meal comes together in a flash for a quick mid-week meal. Add some pan-fried veggies and grilled meat or fish for a balanced meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package pasta (400 grams) pasta, any shape
  • 1 package (227 grams) plain or flavoured cream cheese
  • ½ cup shredded cheese
  • ¼ cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or vegetable oil
  • 3 - 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • Optional: 1/4-1/2 tsp chili flakes, adjust to your taste
  • Optional: 2 cups lightly pan - fried veggies like: mushrooms, spinach leaves, chopped peppers, frozen peas

Preparation:

  • Step 1: In a large pot of boiling water, cook the pasta according to package directions. Right before draining, set aside 1 cup of the starchy pasta water.
  • Step 2: Drain and place back into the same pot.
  • Step 3: Meanwhile in a large skillet, warm the oil on low-medium heat. Add the garlic and stir constantly, do not let the garlic burn.
  • Step 4: Once the garlic is light brown, stir in the cream cheese and about half of the starchy pasta water. Stir and remove lumps until the sauce is smooth.
  • Step 5: Add the smooth sauce to the pasta and turn on the heat to low-medium.
  • Step 6: Add the rest of the starchy pasta water, the cheese, the chili flakes (optional) and the vegetables (optional).
  • Step 7: Cook just until the cheese is melted. Stir well to evenly coat the pasta in the sauce.
  • Step 8: Serve right away. Enjoy!
